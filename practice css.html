Medicinal plants

Parts of plant or the whole plant which are widely used as medicine are medicinal plants.Herbs and spices have been used as medicine from pre historic period. Basil, turmeric, ginger, garlic and coriander are common spices used in our kitchen in our daily life.

Importance of medicinal plants

- Medicinal plants are all natural so they are more beneficial to our health.
-Medicinal plants are less toxic or it has less side effects.
-It is affordable.
